Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel anytime? ▾

Yes. Pro Monthly cancels immediately and you keep access through the end of the current billing period. Pro Annual is non-refundable after the first 7 days.

Do you offer refunds? ▾

Yes — within 7 days of your first charge, no questions asked. Email luke@dawncrestconsulting.com.

How accurate is the data? ▾

Data is sourced directly from SEC EDGAR Form D filings. We parse the official primary_doc.xml for each filing and update continuously. If you spot an error, email me and I'll fix it.

How often is it updated? ▾

New filings typically appear within a few hours of being published on EDGAR.

Can I expense this? ▾

Yes — invoices are emailed automatically and include line items suitable for expense reports. For team or annual invoicing, email luke@dawncrestconsulting.com.

Do you offer team plans? ▾

Not yet, but reach out if you need access for 3+ people and we'll work something out.
